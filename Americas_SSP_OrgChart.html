Career Path & Progression Philosophy
S&SP team career development overview — click to expand
▼

Across all pillars and roles within Americas S&SP, we prioritize the effective execution of individual responsibilities, teamwork, and professional growth. Career progression can manifest in various ways, both within and beyond the S&SP organization.

  • Within team: Advance by taking on a role with a business need for a more senior-level resource within your functional team.
  • Cross-pillar: Move across S&SP pillars to seek different challenges and leverage skills developed in prior roles.
  • Beyond S&SP: Explore opportunities in other Clients & Industries pillars, CBS, or client service — S&SP tenure builds skills and relationships that position you for success anywhere.

Note: The role-specific career paths shown in the Roles view are natural progressions — not absolute or comprehensive. Progression is based on individual interest, skills, and readiness.

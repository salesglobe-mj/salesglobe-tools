AI at the Manager Level

A proposed framework for how Managers apply AI in daily project work — offered as a starting point for the broader AI-integration mapping leadership raised this year. Click an area to see it in practice.

Use AI to turn interview notes and workshop transcripts into first-draft findings and readout structure — shifting a Manager's time from transcription to judgment: shaping the narrative, pressure-testing the recommendation, and deciding what the client actually needs to hear.

Use AI to roll multiple concurrent workstreams into a single status view, surfacing risks and blockers early rather than manually compiling updates from each stream. This is what makes owning several engagements at once — as with First Command and the EY Sales Certification project — sustainable rather than additive overhead.

Use AI to accelerate first-draft scoping language, level-of-effort estimates, and competitive framing — shortening proposal turnaround so more time goes to client-specific customization and commercial judgment, not first-draft assembly.

This is a starting point, not a finished map — intended to be pressure-tested and expanded alongside other Managers as part of the firm's broader AI-integration effort.
